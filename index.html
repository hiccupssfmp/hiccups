Pyrofishing

PyroFishing is a popular Minecraft plugin that enhances the fishing experience by adding custom fish, fishing levels, entropy, augments, and fishing totems. It aims to provide a more engaging and feature-rich fishing system within the game, going beyond the basic vanilla fishing.

Custom Fish: Introduces a variety of unique fish with different tiers and associated XP for fishing level progression.

Fishing Level: Players gain experience and level up their fishing skill by catching custom fish, unlocking new possibilities.

Entropy: A resource used in crafting augments for fishing rods, adding special effects and enhancements.

Augments: "Enchants" for fishing rods that can be crafted with ingredients and entropy, offering various advantages.

Fishing Totems: Advanced structures that can be built to further enhance fishing capabilities, requiring specific ingredients and entropy.

/mine menu

Pyromining

Custom Enchantments: PyroMining introduces custom enchantments for pickaxes, allowing for unique and powerful mining abilities.

Artifacts: The plugin enables the identification and collection of artifacts, which can be found while mining or obtained from other sources.

Vessels: Vessels are custom enchantments that can be applied to pickaxes, and can be crafted using materials obtained through the plugin's features.

Runes: Players can obtain runes by defeating rune guardians, which are creatures that appear after using rune dust on redstone ore.

Refinery: The refinery is a contraption where players can process mysterious dust, which is a byproduct of mining, into other useful items, like rune dust.

Rune Guardians: These are bosses that can be summoned by using rune dust on redstone ore and drop runes upon defeat.

Leveling Singularity: Runes can be used to level up the "singularity," which unlocks various perks and abilities for the player. Overall, PyroMining transforms the vanilla mining experience into a more engaging and rewarding activity with its unique features and progression system.

Lands

The Lands plugin for Minecraft servers is a land management and land/nation simulation tool. It allows players to create lands, claim and protect land, and form nations, offering a structured environment for community building and territorial control. It's a great way to add depth and role-playing elements to your claims.

Jobs 💼

Jobs – Earn Money Doing What You Love! Tired of being broke on the server? With the Jobs system, you can start making money just by doing what you're already good at! Whether you're mining, building, fishing, or hunting mobs — there's a job for you.

🧱 How it works: You can join up to a few jobs at once (depending on server limits). As you play and do actions related to your job, you’ll automatically earn money, experience, and job levels.

🛠️ Examples:
Miner – Get paid for breaking ores and stone.
Fisherman – Earn money with every catch.
Hunter – Slay mobs for cash rewards.
Farmer – Harvest crops and get paid.
Builder – Place blocks to earn income.

The more you work in a job, the more you level up, and higher levels usually mean better pay.
